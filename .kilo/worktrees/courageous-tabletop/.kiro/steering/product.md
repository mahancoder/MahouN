# Mahoun Platform

Mahoun is a zero-hallucination AI reasoning platform designed for high-stakes decisions in regulated industries.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Evidence-Linked Reasoning**: Every conclusion is explicitly linked to evidence in a knowledge graph
- **Zero Hallucination Guarantee**: Graph-based reasoning ensures 100% groundedness
- **Full Auditability**: Complete audit trails for regulatory compliance
- **Deterministic Contradiction Resolution**: Handles conflicting information predictably

## Target Domains

- Healthcare compliance (HIPAA, FDA)
- Financial services (AML detection, regulatory compliance)
- Legal tech (contract analysis, case law reasoning)
- Aerospace & defense (safety-critical decisions)
- Pharmaceuticals (drug interactions, clinical trials)

## Key Invariants

- **I1**: 100% groundedness - every reasoning step must link to graph evidence
- All verdicts must be reproducible and auditable
- Contradictions must be detected and resolved deterministically
